Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bloom identify plant diseases?
Bloom uses a CNN model to recognize the disease your plant has. Our database contains 38 classes of diseases, such as Leaf Scortch, LateBlight, Bacterial and many more...
Will more plant species or diseases be added?
Yes, the application can be updated with additional plant species and diseases as new datasets become available.Feel free to contact us through sources provided in this page if any new disease other than the dataset are found or available so that in further updates it will be added to the application
